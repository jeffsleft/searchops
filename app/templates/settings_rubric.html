{# ── LLM Qualitative Layer ───────────────────────────────────── #}

LLM Qualitative Adjustment (±1.0)

Gemini 2.5 Flash reads the full job description to find signals that raw data misses. It applies a ±1.0 nudge based on the following logic:

Nudge Up (+0.5 to +1.0)
Evidence of high autonomy, "Buy and Build" philosophy, or a direct link between operations and NRR/Consumption.
Nudge Down (-0.5 to -1.0)
Signs of a "firefighter" role, marketing-heavy focus (copywriting/events), or leadership churn/instability.
Example Analysis: Veradata
"The JD focuses heavily on client-facing presentations and IC-level marketing work. While the title is leadership, the daily tasks are tactical. **Nudge: -1.0** (IC-Heavy signal)."

Hard Filters (Auto-Reject)

  • Salary Floor: Auto-reject if base < $120k.
  • Commute: Auto-reject if in-office daily or weekly.
  • Geography: SF commute (2x/week) requires $275k+ base salary.
  • Sectors: Gambling, Predatory Lending, Healthcare (default), and Government.
  • Ethics: Must be manually vetted; auto-rejected if ethical red flags are detected.
