How to use this app

Adding jobs to evaluate

There are two ways to get jobs into the system:

  1. Score a Job form (primary). Use the "Score a Job" form on the Dashboard — paste a URL or raw JD text. Scored immediately.
  2. Discovery scan. The scheduler runs daily at 6am UTC and scans ATS boards for tracked companies, scoring any new roles automatically.
Outreach Targets vs. Jobs

Outreach Targets (/companies) are companies you want to cold-reach — sourced from Crunchbase, no open role yet. Add them here to track research and outreach status. They are not scored jobs.

Jobs (Dashboard, Pipeline) are scored JDs with a specific open role. These come in through the Score a Job form or discovery scan.

Scoring system

Scores are 0–10. Two layers:

  • Deterministic layer — hard rules: dealbreakers (comp floor, geography, sector blacklist), weighted criteria (greenfield scope, tech stack, company stage). Runs first; auto-rejects before touching the LLM.
  • LLM adjustment — Gemini reads the full JD and adjusts ±2 points based on qualitative signals not captured by the rules.

Interview prep workflow
  1. Move a job to Researching — this seeds the question bank automatically.
  2. Go to Interview Prep for that job to see your opening hook, top unanswered questions, and anchor stories.
  3. Before each interview: generate a Cheat Sheet (round + interviewer → one-pager).
  4. After: paste the transcript into Analyze Transcript or Interview Coaching for scoring and debrief.
  5. Use Interviewer Research to get a brief on who you're meeting before the call.
